Predicted stock movement using Stocktwits Text Data. I organized the StockTwits text data into TF-IDF metrics, moving averages, and polarities. I restructured the dataframe using simple inner joins and adjusted t-day price changes into binary (up or down). I conducted Naive-Bayes and Logistic Regression classification on whether the stock price would go up or down based on term frequencies. High accuracy for training data but extremely overfit as test accuracy was low. Next, I applied ordinary linear regression on the continuous t-day price change. Again, extremely successful training accuracy and R^2, but overfit when it came to test dataset.
